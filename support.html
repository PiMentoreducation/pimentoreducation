❓ Common Questions

How do I track my progress? +
Your progress is automatically saved when you watch 85% of a video or pass a lecture quiz. Check the 'Curriculum' sidebar in the player.
Math symbols aren't loading? +
Wait 1-2 seconds for MathJax to render. If it still doesn't show, refresh the page to re-trigger the rendering engine.
Can I access PDF notes offline? +
Yes, once you open the PDF resource in the player, you can use the 'Download' icon in the top right of the viewer.
